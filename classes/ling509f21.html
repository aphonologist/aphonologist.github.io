Description

This course introduces students to computational models of language and the Python programming language. We will pursue two strands: (1) formal constructs that have been very productive in the study of language such as finite-state machines and context-free grammars and (2) a computational platform to implement these constructs programmatically. Our discussion of these strands will be intertwined and motivated by linguistic questions.
